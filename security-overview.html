TAMPER PROOF

Immutable Cryptographic Seals

Every signature event generates a mathematically unique seal binding the signer's identity, the exact timestamp, and the document's binary structure.

  • Instant Detection: Altering a single pixel breaks the digital signature universally.
  • Non-Repudiation: Cryptographic evidence prevents signers from denying involvement.
  • Long-Term Validation: Proofs utilize standard algorithms (SHA-256, RSA-4096) that survive platform lifecycles.
