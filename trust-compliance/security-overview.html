Four Pillars of BrillSign Security

Each pillar reinforces the others, creating a defense-in-depth architecture that eliminates single points of failure.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We verify documents using cryptographic fingerprints — never the actual content. Your data is architecturally invisible to us.

End-to-End Encryption

AES-256 encryption happens on your device before data ever touches our servers. Keys are never transmitted or stored by BrillSign.

Decentralized Storage

Encrypted document shards are distributed across multiple independent nodes. No single breach can expose your data.

Independent Verification

Signatures can be verified by any third party using open cryptographic proofs — no dependency on BrillSign's systems.
