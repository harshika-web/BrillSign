What “Encrypted eSign” Actually Means?

Most eSign platforms store a signed PDF and a log saying it was approved. Brillsign mathematically seals what you sign.

Mathematical Sealing

Document binary content is sealed using SHA-256 hashes.

Version Linking

Every signature is permanently bound to that exact version.

Immediate Detectability

Any future alteration is immediately detectable by anyone.
