Getting Started in 5 Steps

Follow this quick guide to go from zero to your first signed document.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email or SSO. No credit card required for the free tier.

Takes 30 seconds
2

Upload a Document

Drag & drop or browse to upload your PDF, DOCX, or image file. It's encrypted on your device instantly.

Client-side encrypted
3

Add Signers

Enter email addresses and set the signing order. Add signature fields by dragging them onto the document.

Supports multiple signers
4

Send for Signing

Hit send and each signer gets a secure link. They can sign from any device — no account needed.

Instant delivery
5

Download & Verify

Once all parties sign, download the completed document with its cryptographic proof and audit trail.

Independently verifiable
