The Lifecycle of a Secure Record

1. Document Generation & Hashing

Forms are generated from your EHR. Instead of storing the document, BrillSign computes a secure cryptographic hash (a digital fingerprint) of the file locally.

2. Patient Identity Verification

The patient authenticates via secure link or biometric device integrated into the clinical workflow, binding their digital identity to the specific hash.

3. Immutable Anchoring

The signed hash is broadcasted to our decentralized network, creating a permanent timestamp and proof of consent that cannot be altered retroactively.
