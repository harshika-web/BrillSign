BrillSign and Anthropic: Bringing Intelligent Agreement Workflows to Cowork

Agreement management is essential to how businesses operate, yet much of the process still relies on manual, repetitive work spread across systems and teams. Today, organizations are turning to agentic AI that not only answers questions but takes action — automating agreement workflows that once required hours of human effort.

We're excited to announce BrillSign's partnership with Anthropic to bring Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) into Claude Cowork. By connecting to BrillSign in Cowork, businesses can securely create, review, send, and manage agreements from start to finish — all through natural language prompts in Claude.

With this connector, teams can move directly from a prompt to a workflow. AI goes beyond summaries and suggestions to trigger real-time actions across legal, sales, procurement, HR, and finance.

What teams can accomplish

Here are just a few examples of what teams can accomplish with BrillSign's intelligent agreement workflows:

  • Create a contract from a BrillSign-provided Master Service Agreement template, populate business details, and route it to Legal
  • Send a customer intake packet and receive notification when identity verification is complete
  • Review AI-suggested redlines to a service contract and initiate a vendor review aligned to company policy
  • Search for customer contracts expiring in the next 90 days that include a price increase clause
  • Find all active contracts containing a "data protection" clause and export a summary report

Each action is backed by BrillSign's IAM platform, ensuring workflows follow defined rules, permissions, and compliance requirements — all with end-to-end encryption and zero-knowledge architecture at the core.

Manage agreements where work happens

Teams increasingly embrace AI tools to plan, analyze, and coordinate work. But agreement execution happens outside of AI tools that organizations are using — forcing people to switch tools or manually hand off next steps.

By integrating BrillSign directly into Cowork, teams can run agreement workflows without leaving their AI workspace. The result is faster execution, fewer handoffs, and less operational friction.

"We stopped thinking about signatures as a workflow and started treating them as evidence. BrillSign gave us confidence that every signed document would still hold up months or years later."

Built for enterprise trust

The BrillSign connector for Cowork is built on the Model Context Protocol (MCP) and uses BrillSign's trusted security and access controls. Customers must be licensed and authenticated, access is permission-based, and agreement data remains private and under the user's control.

Unlike traditional e-signature vendors, BrillSign's architecture ensures that no one — not even BrillSign — can access your document contents. Our zero-knowledge design means your sensitive agreements are cryptographically sealed from everyone, including us.

Availability

The BrillSign MCP connector is available in beta today through Anthropic's Connectors Directory for BrillSign customers.
