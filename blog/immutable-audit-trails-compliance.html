For most enterprises, audit season is a predictable crisis. Teams scramble to compile records, cross-reference access logs, and reconstruct approval chains that should have been documented months ago. The process is expensive, stressful, and fundamentally reactive.

BrillSign takes the opposite approach. Every action — every signature, every view, every access decision — is cryptographically sealed the moment it occurs. When auditors arrive, the evidence is already compiled, verified, and mathematically provable.

The audit anxiety problem

A 2025 survey by Compliance Week found that 73% of compliance officers describe audit preparation as "significantly disruptive" to normal operations. The average mid-market enterprise spends 680 hours annually just preparing documentation for regulatory reviews.

The root cause isn't a lack of records — it's a lack of trustworthy records. When audit logs can be edited, backdated, or selectively curated, auditors can't trust them. They must independently verify each record, which multiplies the time and cost of every review.

"Our last SOC2 audit completed 60% faster because auditors could cryptographically verify every record instead of manually cross-referencing logs. The immutability isn't just compliance — it's efficiency."

Immutable by design, not by promise

BrillSign's audit trails aren't immutable because we promise not to change them. They're immutable because changing them is cryptographically impossible. Every record is hashed and chained to its predecessors, creating a Merkle tree structure where altering any single entry would invalidate the entire chain.

This means even BrillSign's own administrators cannot modify, delete, or selectively redact audit entries. The integrity is mathematical, not procedural.

What BrillSign captures — automatically

Every document interaction generates a comprehensive forensic record:

Signer Identity Proofs

Cryptographic identity verification records — who signed, how they authenticated, and when.

Timestamp Chains

RFC 3161-compliant timestamps from trusted authorities, chained to prevent backdating.

Access Logs

Every document view, download, and share event — with IP, device, and geolocation metadata.

Version History

Complete document revision history with diff tracking and approval chains.

Compliance as a competitive edge

Forward-thinking enterprises are discovering that best-in-class compliance isn't just a cost of doing business — it's a competitive differentiator. When you can demonstrate to prospects, partners, and regulators that your document handling is cryptographically verifiable, you gain trust that competitors can't easily replicate.

  • Faster customer onboarding: Enterprise buyers increasingly require SOC2/ISO certification before signing contracts. Instant audit evidence accelerates sales cycles
  • Insurance advantages: Cyber insurers offer premium reductions for organizations with provably immutable audit trails
  • M&A readiness: During due diligence, verifiable signing history reduces risk assessments and can increase valuation
  • Regulatory relationships: Proactive, provable compliance builds goodwill with regulators, reducing the likelihood of invasive investigations
