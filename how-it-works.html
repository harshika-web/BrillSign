The Cryptographic Signing Lifecycle

Six steps to absolute certainty. No platform trust required.

01

Secure Ingestion

Your document is uploaded to our secure environment where it is prepared for the cryptographic sealing process.

02

Digital Fingerprinting

A SHA-256 hash is generated — the digital DNA unique to your document. Even a single pixel change breaks the match.

03

Identity Verification

The signer's identity is verified through multi-factor authentication or decentralized identifiers before signing access is granted.

04

Cryptographic Binding

The signer's approval is mathematically fused with the document's fingerprint using RSA-4096 encryption — unbreakable by design.

05

Distributed Anchoring

The signed evidence is anchored across a decentralized node network — no single entity, including BrillSign, can alter the history.

06

Eternal Verifiability

The document becomes a self-contained proof. It can be verified by anyone, anywhere, at any time — even if our platform disappears.
