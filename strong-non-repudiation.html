Technical Workflow

How It Works (Evidence Chain)

BrillSign cryptographically binds identity, intent, and content into an immutable proof of non-repudiation that remains valid for decades.

Phase 01: Identification
01

Identity verified

Signers are authenticated using high-assurance identity methods, ensuring that the person signing is who they claim to be.

Biometric Scan eIDAS G2
brillsign.com/verify-identity
Phase 02: Cryptography
02

Document fingerprint created

A cryptographic hash of the document is generated. This fingerprint is unique to the exact content, making any future edit detectable.

algorithm: SHA-256 state: immutable
brillsign.com/hashing-engine
Phase 03: Binding
03

Signature cryptographically bound

The identity, the document fingerprint, and the timestamp are cryptographically bound together into a single, permanent record of intent.

Atomic binding protocol: trustchain™
brillsign.com/binding-protocol
Phase 04: Anchoring
04

Proof permanently recorded

The resulting proof is anchored to a decentralized ledger, ensuring that it remains valid and verifiable for decades, independent of any platform.

storage: ledger validity: permanent
brillsign.com/anchoring
