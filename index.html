Tamper-Proof Audit Trails Every action is cryptographically sealed the moment it occurs, no silent edits, no retroactive changes, no ambiguity. When regulators, auditors, or legal teams examine your records, the integrity is mathematically provable, not just promised.

Compliance Built Into the Workflow Compliance isn't a checklist, it's embedded in the architecture. Data residency, retention policies, and access controls are enforced automatically as documents flow through your organization, eliminating human error and reducing the daily compliance burden on your teams.

Zero-Knowledge Data Handling We're architecturally incapable of reading your documents, by design, not by policy. This eliminates insider threats, internal exposure, and vendor-trust dependencies entirely. Your confidential data remains yours, cryptographically sealed from everyone, including us.

Jurisdiction-Aware Signing As documents cross borders, signing workflows adapt automatically to meet local regulatory requirements, GDPR in Europe, eIDAS compliance, or region-specific mandates. You avoid compliance gaps without bottlenecking transactions or escalating every deal to legal review.

Privacy-Preserving Identity Verification Identity verification happens without surveillance, behavioral tracking, or persistent user profiles. You get cryptographic certainty about who signed, without creating privacy risks, regulatory liabilities, or long-term data exposure for your signers.

Exit-Safe Architecture Your documents outlive any vendor relationship. If you migrate platforms or your needs evolve, every signature remains independently verifiable without re-signing contracts or maintaining our service. Legal validity is permanent, not contingent on our existence.
