Key Regulatory Frameworks

BrillSign enforces the correct technical standard automatically depending on where your signers are located.

🇪🇺

eIDAS — European Union

QES / AES / SES

The gold standard for electronic signatures. QES carries the same legal weight as a handwritten signature across all 27 member states.

🇺🇸

ESIGN & UETA — United States

Federal + State

ESIGN Act (federal) and UETA (state-level) provide broad enforceability for consumer and commercial e-signatures across the US.

🇬🇧

UK eIDAS — United Kingdom

Post-Brexit

UK retained its own eIDAS-equivalent framework post-Brexit. QES issued by UKAS-accredited Trust Service Providers is fully valid.

🇨🇭

ZertES — Switzerland

QES Mandatory

Switzerland's ZertES law mandates accredited Certificate Authorities for legally binding QES, fully supported by BrillSign's integration layer.

🇮🇳

IT Act — India

DSC Required (Tier 3)

High-value transactions require Aadhaar-based or PKI token Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) recognised under the Information Technology Act, 2000.
