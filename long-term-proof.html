If you migrate away from a traditional e-sign vendor, you lose access to the native audit trail. BrillSign's proof is embedded in the PDF, remaining verifiable offline.

Future-Proof Algorithmics

As cryptography evolves, BrillSign supports hash upgrading and cryptographic timestamping designed for long-term archival standards (LTV/PAdES).

Purpose-Built for the Archive

Blockchain & Ledger Anchoring

Document proofs are rolled up and anchored into immutable public ledgers. This creates an unforgeable timestamp proving the document existed at a specific moment in history.

Decentralized Storage (IPFS)

Optionally distribute secure, encrypted fragments of your audit logs across decentralized networks, removing the single point of failure inherent in cloud providers.

PAdES LTV Compliance

Signatures conform to the strictly defined Long Term Validation profiles, ensuring they meet the technical requirements for multi-decade archiving required by EU courts.
