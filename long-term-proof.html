Digital Longevity FAQ

Technical insights into century-scale document integrity.

Standard e-signatures depend on the validity of certificates at the time of signing. PAdES-LTV embeds all necessary revocation data (CRLs/OCSP) and a qualified timestamp into the document itself, allowing it to be verified decades later without contacting the original Certificate Authority.
