How Decentralized Document Control Reduces Organizational Risk

Decentralized document control doesn’t change how you send or sign agreements. It changes what happens when something goes wrong — an audit, a breach, an outage, or a dispute.

Instead of relying on a provider to confirm events, your organization retains independent proof and continuous access to its agreements.
