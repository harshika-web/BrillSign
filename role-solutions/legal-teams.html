Document Lifecycle

From Draft to Unbreakable Evidence

Every contract signed on BrillSign goes through a cryptographic sealing process the moment a signer touches it. Here's what that journey looks like.

01

Document Upload & Fingerprinting

Your document is hashed with SHA-256 the moment it enters BrillSign's system. This hash is your document's immutable fingerprint — change one byte and the hash breaks.

02

Signer Identity Capture

We bind each signature to multi-factor identity evidence: email, device fingerprint, geolocation, and timestamp — creating an identity chain no one can later deny.

03

Cryptographic Seal Applied

The signature is merged with the document hash under a PKI cryptographic seal. Any subsequent alteration of the document invalidates this seal — instantly detectable.

04

Audit Log Frozen & Stored

A tamper-proof audit log containing every action, timestamp, and identity data point is frozen and stored in decentralized infrastructure, ready for court disclosure at any time.
