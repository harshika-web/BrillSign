Zero-Knowledge Identity Flow

From biometric to cryptographic proof

BrillSign verifies identity through a four-stage pipeline. The result is a zero-knowledge proof token — a cryptographic assertion that proves identity without ever exposing the underlying personal data to BrillSign or any third party.

Government ID verified

Zero-Knowledge Privacy Guarantee

We never store raw biometric data. The identity verification pipeline generates a tamper-proof ZK proof — which cryptographically proves who you are without retaining any sensitive personal information.

No PII stored on BrillSign servers GDPR Article 89 — compliant by design Local device verification only

Biometric Capture

Face scan, fingerprint, or government ID verification through a certified identity provider (KYC/AML).

ZK Proof Generation

A zero-knowledge proof is derived from the identity data — proving authenticity without revealing the raw PII.

Trust Token Issued

A cryptographic trust token is issued for this session only — it cannot be reused, transferred, or spoofed.

Signature Binding

The token is permanently bound to the signature — becoming an unalterable proof of who signed what, and when.
